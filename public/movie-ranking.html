The Best Books According to Fatima

My top 3 books of all time

Jannat Kay Pattay

An amazing novel that explores the life of Haya and Jihan as they encounter numerous problems and challenges in their life. And here is a beautiful quote from the book:

"Shroo mein Islam ajnabi tha
Aur anqareeb phir ajnabi ho jae ga
Salam ho un ajnabiyon par"

Peer e Kamil

A must to read novel that revolves around Imama and Salaar as they find the right path.

Aab e Hiyaat

This is the second part of Peer e Kamil that dives deeper into the life of Imama and Salaar as they embark on their journey for life.
